About Us

We are part of an Innovation Project titled “Innovation in Payment System: Strategies to Convert Unbanked Into Banked” issued to Maharaja Agrasen College, Vasundhra Enclave by the University of Delhi.Our project focuses on financial inclusion of rural and semi-rural areas. We have done an extensive study of all the payment systems that currently exist and function in the market and how they affect the people. Based on this study we have proposed a better system that will not only optimize the payment experience of the users but will also encourage transparent payment transactions.

Our Mission is to introduce a new, efficient and secure payment system in the Indian Mandis and help people transition from the old and existing payment methods to the new better one or to introduce a new payment system in the Indian Mandis by improving the already existing payment system.

Thus, creating a system which is:

  • Online

  • Efficient

  • Secure

  • Easy to use

  • Transparentt
